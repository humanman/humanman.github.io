My journey in film and writing has brought me to amazing places where I have met inspiring people, and now I begin a new chapter in my life. I have found a new medium to explore; within to create; from which to grow.

From simple web games to augmented reality, so much can be gained from learning the basic tools to operate within technology, and I want to use my background in creative development to bring artistry to the work I will be doing in this exciting field.
